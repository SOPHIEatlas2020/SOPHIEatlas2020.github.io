• About

This Evidence Atlas maps studies that research the links between marine environments and positive and negative impacts to human health and well-being.

It has been designed to map research in Europe, North America, Australia and New Zealand, up to May 2018. It does not show evidence from other areas of the world, (Africa, South and Central America and Asia.

Points that are placed in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean are "global" or "multicontintental", and do not have a single location.
